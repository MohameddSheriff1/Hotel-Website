Restaurants

Several outlets around the hotel serve international cuisine in the form of an open buffet or served meals. During your stay, you can taste a wide variety of different meals from different cultures.

Swimming Pools

Seagull has 6 swimming pools including an aqua park. All swimming pools are surrounded by shaded loungers. For drinks, there is a bar by each swimming pool. The aqua park has 3 water slides for adults and 8 for children.

Conference Room

Our meeting rooms provide a beautiful surrounding that supports creativity and success. The two meeting rooms (one with 200sqm and one with 50sqm) ensure a pleasant working atmosphere for up to 200 people.

Clinic

If you feel unwell, we won't let anything ruin your holiday. Doctors are available 24/7 in our clinic.

Entertainment

Our animation team organizes beach and pool games, such as volleyball, bocce, darts, Zumba, or belly dancing classes. Each evening, there is a special program in our amphitheater, live music during dinner, and late-night disco.
